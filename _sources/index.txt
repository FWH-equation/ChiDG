Welcome!
========

**ChiDG: A Chimera-based, discontinuous Galerkin solver**

ChiDG is a Chimera-based framework for solving sets of partial differential 
equations using the discontinuous Galerkin method.

Discretization advantages:
    - Arbitrarily high-order accurate
    - Curved elements for conforming to geometry
    - Chimera overset grids for representing complex geometry

Framework capabilities:
    - Fully-implicit Newton's method
    - Intrinsic automatic differentiation
